Chinese cusinine

Chinese cuisine, deeply rooted in Chinese culture, is one of the world's most diverse culinary traditions, featuring a wide array of regional variations based on ingredients and cooking styles across the country; key staples include rice, noodles, tofu, and soy sauce, often prepared in a wok and eaten with chopsticks, while prominent flavors can range from the fiery spice of Sichuan cuisine to the delicate tastes of Cantonese dishes, making it a globally influential food culture with dishes like dumplings, Peking duck, and spring rolls widely recognized worldwide.
